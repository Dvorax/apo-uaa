2013 Section 8 Conference

Get ready for the Iditarod Dog Sled Race, Fur Rondy Festival, and the Governor's Cup Hockey Game as part of the 2013 Sectional Conference our chapter is hosting March 1-3 in Anchorage, Alaska! The theme is "Keep Calm and Serve on" in light of us surviving the end of the world 2012.

Registration

Register to reserve your spot in the conference! It also helps us get a better idea of the number of people coming so we can plan accordingly and have accomodations for everybody. As you go through the registration, please read the notes along the page. Don't miss the important notices at the top!

Event Listing

Workshops

There are two choices for workshops we will have at the conference:
Discover of APO LEADS
Learn strategies for assertive communication and self-motivation. Remember that you need to take Launch before you can take Discover, so make sure you have by the time of the conference if you are planning to take it.
Non-APO LEADS workshops
There are two workshops taught by members of our own chapter: Safezone Training and Digital Media Reputation Management.

Service Event

We'll be up early to do a group service event until around noon Saturday morning.

Fur Rondy

Fur Rondy is a local festival happening alongside the start of the Iditarod dog sled race.

Sectional Business

Our section has business we must attend to for the conference.

Dinner

Dinner is included in your conference fee and there will be awards!

Governor's Cup

Saturday night ends with a hockey game between UAA and UAF!

Pricing

We are charging $38 per person to attend and a $2 late fee after Feb 15 to encourage everybody to register early. The fee will be used to cover the costs of dinner, T-shirts, snacks, room reservations, etc. Everybody can pay with cash or a check at the conference itself, but there may be the option to pay online or with a credit card in the near future.
